    Ken Rolston once said, 'Tell God's story, then tell the farmer's story, then listen to what the dog has to say.', and I wholeheartedly believe it. As an experienced Narrative Designer with a passion for immersive storytelling, I'm well-versed in the art of bringing stories to life, creating experiences that are engaging, immersive, and thought-provoking. Video games have a unique power to connect with players on an emotional level, and contributing to this exciting and ever-evolving medium has always been a childhood dream for me.

    After graduating from the Università di Bologna as a Master of Computer Science, I have expanded my skills further with proficiency in website development and big data analytics. Currently, I am tinkering about with Bootstrap and jQuery to complement my skills as a Front-End Engineer. Moreover, I am also quite adapt in Python by utilizing both Pandas (for specialized dataset) and SQL (for big data analysis) as the base for my data analytics.
